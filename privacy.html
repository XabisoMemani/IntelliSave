Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

IntelliSave requires permissions to manage downloads, store settings, read the active tab URL and show notifications. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing history. All information (download URLs, rules) remains within the user’s browser and is used only to sort files.

No personally‑identifiable information, health information, financial data, authentication credentials, personal communications, location, web history, user activity, or website content is ever sent outside of the browser. No data is sold or transferred to third parties.

By installing or using IntelliSave you agree that the extension will operate in accordance with this policy. Changes to this policy will be posted at this page and reflected in the Chrome Web Store listing.
